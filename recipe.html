Recipe for Spahetti Bolognese

This is a recipe for Spaghetti bolognese without cheese!

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Spaghetti
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Carrots
  • Salt and pepper
  • Oil
  • Bayleafs

Steps

  1. Heat some water with salt, pebber and oil, and put in Spaghetti when the water boils
  2. Cut the garlic and onions
  3. Heat the meat, garlic and onions
  4. While heating, cut the carrots
  5. When meat is brown, put in the tomatoes, carrots and bayleaves
  6. Taste with salt and pebber
  7. Wait for spagetti to finish cooking and then serve
